MOVIE REVIEW Django

Directed By:Bryan Singer

Running Time: 130 Minutes

RATING:

Django Unchained

Django Unchained a group of unlikely animal friends embarks on a thrilling and hilarious adventure. Manny, a grumpy yet kind-hearted woolly mammoth, finds himself unexpectedly becoming the reluctant leader of the group. Sid, a talkative and clumsy sloth, and Diego, a fearless and cunning saber-toothed tiger, join Manny on his journey. As they navigate through icy landscapes, treacherous caves, and encounter various prehistoric creatures, the trio forms an unlikely bond and learns the true meaning of friendship. Along the way, they encounter Scrat, a persistent and acorn-obsessed squirrel, whose comedic escapades add an extra layer of hilarity to their adventure.
