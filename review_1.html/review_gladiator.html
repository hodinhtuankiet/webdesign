MOVIE REVIEW GLADIATOR

Directed By:Bryan Singer

Running Time: 130 Minutes

RATING:

GLADIATOR: Strength,Honor,Redemption

"Gladiator" is an epic historical drama film directed by Ridley Scott, released in 2000. Set in ancient Rome, the film tells the story of Maximus Decimus Meridius, a powerful general who is betrayed and his family murdered by Commodus, the corrupt Emperor of Rome. Reduced to slavery, Maximus rises through the ranks as a gladiator and seeks revenge against Commodus while also becoming a symbol of hope and freedom for the Roman people.The film explores themes of honor, loyalty, sacrifice, and the pursuit of justice. It showcases the brutal and gladiatorial world of ancient Rome, depicting intense battles in the Colosseum
